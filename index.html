Recipe for PB & J

A simple and deliscious sandwich. A true lunch classic.

The finished product.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly or Jam

Instructions

  1. Lay out two slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanutbutter evenly across ose side of one slice of bread.
  3. Spread jelly evenly across one side of the other slice of bread.
  4. Press the two slices of bread togheter.
